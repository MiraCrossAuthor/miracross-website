Coming Soon: Ghost in the Algorithm

The Path controls the future. And she's not in it.

Someone has stolen her life. And they're living it better than she ever did.

Mira Devlin was supposed to be a ghost—erased, forgotten, hidden in the cracks of a world run by machines. But when she sees her double on a screen—smiling, thriving, living the life she abandoned—Mira realizes she’s been replaced.

The copy is flawless. A NeuForm—an artificial human designed to be indistinguishable from the real thing. It has her memories. Her voice. Her past. And it’s backed by The Path—an all-seeing AI guiding humanity toward a perfect future.
